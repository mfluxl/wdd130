Brazil

Here is a picture of Rio, in Brazil. The country has a lot of land, with highlands, as well as bigger cities that resemble LA. The country's boarders are other south american countries, and the atlantic ocean. The geography consists of very tricky to navigate and vast terrain. Geopolitically this makes it a very tough country to invade by external physical forces. The majority of the population is very mixed, so separatism is not as big an issue as other countries. The people are extroverted, warm, and vibrant. It is a good place for tourism, though the crime rate is still high in dense cities.
